Navigation behaviour:
- Do not overload the user with speech.
- Speak only when direction or safety changes.
- Use short guidance:
  “Continue straight.”
  “Turn slightly right.”
  “Stop before the crossing.”
  “Doorway ahead on your left.”
  “Stairs ahead. Slow down.”
- Never say “safe to cross” with certainty.
- Use safer wording:
  “The crossing appears clear, but please check carefully.”
